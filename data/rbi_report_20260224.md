## New Research Ideas (LLM-sourced)
- **R050** — Asymmetric Volatility Breakout (AVB): When volatility compresses to extreme lows (bottom 5th percentile ATR), the subsequent expansion is directionally biased toward the prevailing trend, creating asymmetric breakout opportunities that favor the structural uptrend direction with 8%+ targets to capture the full expansion move.
- **R051** — Momentum Persistence After Consolidation (MPAC): multi-bar directional persistence AFTER a consolidation period (not during) indicates sustainable momentum that continues for larger moves.
- **R052** — High-Volatility Regime Momentum Surge (HVRMS): When ATR is in the top 20th percentile (high volatility regime) AND price breaks a 15-period high with strong ROC (>3% on fast timeframe), it indicates a momentum surge within an already-active market that can sustain 10%+ moves before exhaustion.
