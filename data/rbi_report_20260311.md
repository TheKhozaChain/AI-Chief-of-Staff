## New Research Ideas (LLM-sourced)
- **R098** — Deep Pullback Trend Re-Entry (DPTRE): In a structural BTC uptrend, deeper pullbacks (3-5%) that then show momentum bounce off a rising MA represent higher-conviction re-entries where the trend has been stress-tested and buyers are absorbing supply.
- **R099** — RSI Oversold Bounce in Strong Uptrend (ROBU): When BTC is in a confirmed uptrend (price above slow MA) and RSI dips into oversold territory (below 35), the subsequent bounce captures mean-reversion of momentum within a trending regime, providing asymmetric risk/reward.
- **R100** — ATR Regime-Scaled Trend Continuation (ARSTC): BTC volatility is highly regime-dependent — by scaling stop and target distances proportionally to the current ATR percentile rank, the strategy stays in trades long enough during explosive moves while protecting capital during choppy low-vol periods.
